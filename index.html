About Mantz

On this page you will be told about Mantz.


The reason this page is here is so you can get to know mantz a little bit without having to watch too many of his videos.

First of all welcome to the website AND The Colony.
The guy you hear talking in the videos is Man of the Antz or Mantz for short.

He owns the Oculus rift and Touch which is why most of the channel is VR content.
Some are quick looks at interesting titles other are let's plays on longer story based games

Anything goes on the Oculus so he'll be playing through shooters, RPGs, horror, strategy, and everything in between.
He will be doing some series for some non-VR games as well, as and when they take his fancy.
That will mainly revolve around strategy & simulation games such as Planet Coaster, Cities Skylines,
and Farming Simulator. Saying that, if something does crop up this will likely expand to:
shooters, RPGs or action games!
He also does a video AND stream wednesday at 6pm UK time (GMT)
